Setup Guide — Formspree (Free)
Your site is static — there's no server to process form submissions. Formspree acts as the middleman: it receives the email, stores it, and forwards it to your inbox. Free plan handles up to 50 submissions/month.
  1. Go to formspree.io and create a free account.
  2. Create a new form. Give it a name like "0served Newsletter" and set your email address as the recipient.
  3. Copy your form's Form ID — it looks like xabcdefg (8 characters).
  4. Paste it into the field below and click Save Newsletter Settings.
  5. Download or save site-content.json and upload it to your server. That's it — the subscription form on your site will now work!
